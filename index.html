Different Species

There are many different types of killer bunny, with these being the 5 most common.

  • Western Colar Bunny

    • The Western Colar Bunny is your average killer bunny, varying in size. These killer bunnys
    like disguising themselves as little children, because children are fast.

  • Southern Colar Bunny

    • The smallest (but most durable) of the killer bunnies are the Southern Colar Bunnies. They are
    often found assisting the quacker jacks and helping them get away with their newest invention.

  • Screaming Colar Bunny

    • The most vocal killer bunny, these killer bunnies disguise as small screaming children and scream
    in your ear to divert your attention. Grandpa loves to hunt them down with his machine gun.

  • Boss Colar Bunny

    • Boss Colar Bunnys are big and strong, and they disguise themselves as police and supervisionades
    so the other killer bunnies stay protected. The are also super fast.

  • Disco Colar Bunny

    • Disco Colar Bunnies were discovered very recently by grandpa. All of the types of killer bunny turn
    into Disco Colar Bunnies when they steal grandpa's things.
